Project Overview

The Essavo Development Team is tasked to establish a web application that directs sales to the Essavo cleaning kit. Through the use of transparency and instant gratification, the goal is to increase sales and grow a community. By incorporating short form videos as displayed in modern media, the attention of the user is reeled in to purchase the product. Using modern web development tools and creative ways to market, the success of Essavo will start here.
